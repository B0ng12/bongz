    Welcome to Mpilo Central Hospital

    Mpilo Central Hospital is a 1 000 bedded Hospital. It is a major paternal centre which serves the population of Bulawayo Metropolitan Province, Matabeleland North and South, Midlands and Masvingo Provinces. It is also a teaching hospital for the National University of Science and Technology (NUST), and has strong links with the University of Zimbabwe (UZ) and several other academic institutions. Employs over 2 300 staff across its services.Quality and Safety are at the heart of everything we do.

    About Us

    Mpilo Central Hospital is a 1 000 bedded Hospital. It is a major paternal centre which serves the population of Bulawayo Metropolitan Province, Matabeleland North and South, Midlands and Masvingo Provinces. It is also a teaching hospital for the National University of Science and Technology (NUST), and has strong links with the University of Zimbabwe (UZ) and several other academic institutions. Employs over 2 300 staff across its services.Quality and Safety are at the heart of everything we do.



    Mpilo Central is a referal hospital. For you to be attended you should have referals from other health institutions (Clinics, District Hospitals& Provincial Hospitals)in these Provinces:Matabeleland North and South, Midlands and Masvingo

    • Mission

      • Mpilo Central Hospital delivers a holistic high quality Health Care and Training through the most efficient and innovative use of available resources using Research, Planning and Evidenced Based Strategies

    • Vision

      • Mpilo Central Hospital thrives to be the Centre of excellence in the provision of a holistic health service as well as Training and Research opportunities in Zimbabwe, the Southern Africa

    • Values

      • We strive to achieve Diligence, People Centered Service, Confidentiality, Team work, Accountability and Innovation in all we do.

    • Commitment

      • Mpilo is committed to excellence in patient care, research, education and community service.We are fully committed to providing the highest quality of care to the patients we serve.
